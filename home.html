How it works?

We use data and a few variables to try and predict what the outcome of the match will be. Some data might be missing, you might not be able to select a few specific teams but we use a custom Machine Learning Algorithm so we're just getting started! Some highlights of how we're going to make things better are listed below:
‍
1. Using a larger dataset: We are currently sourcing a larger dataset and should have an update on this soon
2. Use twitter data: We want to see what everyones saying about the teams and incorporate that into our predictions
3. Introducing more variables: We are going to get creative about what things can impact the outcomes. We might even be able to predict how many goals each team scores!

How To Use

You can use this to help you place your bets! We try and best to provide our predictions but CANNOT guarentee anything!

Win/Loss/Draw:
We use two years worth of data to try and predict which team will win or if it will be a draw

Accuracy:
Our accuracy score represents how many times we are accurate over the last two seasons. Randomly picking one of the three would result in a 33% accuracy score. In real cases, humans should have a better prediction then 33% so we wouldn't rely on our algorithim just yet!
